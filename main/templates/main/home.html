Software Engineer

I’ve always been fascinated with computers. But it wasn’t until my mother started calling me every other day for help with her computer that I decided to steer away from a hardware and help-desk route to become a software developer.

I was also frustrated that I couldn’t make software solve the problems that needed to be solved in my previous jobs. I knew that I could create something better - so I started learning to write software instead of merely using it. And yes, I still help fix my mom’s computer when she calls.

Education

I earned my BASc in Software Development from Green River, graduating Summa Cum Laude with a 4.0 GPA. I was nominated for the President’s Award and won the Technology Award, an award given to the top student across eight different departments. The strengths that made me successful as a student also make me a great software engineer: strong logic and math, a knack for debugging, being extremely detail-oriented, following specifications before going above and beyond, and the ability to adapt and be agile.

Skills / Interests

I’m passionate about improving the efficiency of my clients and delivering a solution that meets their unique set of requirements for a perfect piece of software.
